Patachou is pleased to offer several catering options from our restaurants, made with the highest quality ingredients, prepared fresh and to your specifications. Our catering menu is designed for groups of 15 people or more. Groups smaller than 15 can call their local Cafe Patachou to work directly with the manager to fulfill their order. Delivery is available for orders needed after 11am.

Patachou at the Stutz offers a private room available to reserve for celebrations and meetings of 12 or less. Please fill out the "Catering Request" form below to inquire.

Whether it is a custom order or a standard one, please fill out the “Catering Request” form below and a staff member will be in touch with you within 48 hours, Monday through Friday, to start planning. Any requests made over the weekend will be processed Monday morning.
